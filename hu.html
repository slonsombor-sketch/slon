Restaurant Slon is set on the banks of the Great Bačka Canal, nestled in nature. For decades, guests have traveled from Sombor and across the region to enjoy the unique combination of natural surroundings and exceptional gastronomy.

Our kitchen offers a wide selection of national and international specialties — from traditional Serbian grill and fish dishes to à la carte delicacies. Every dish is prepared with care, using only the freshest ingredients.

Our legendary Slonovača — a house specialty passed down through generations — is the reason many guests keep coming back.
